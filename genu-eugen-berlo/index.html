About Genu Berlo

About

Genu

American - born Romanian

working in Bucuresti and Ghirdoveni

In painting, installation, land art and sculpture Genu reflects on ideas such as Magnetic Interaction under Field Awareness and Space-Time-Conscience vortex relations, Fractality of Matter and Mind, Architectural nesting of the Body and embodiment of words, architecture and archetypes. His crossdomain educational backgrounds are of strong influence when it comes to designing structures and spaces embedded with Value Inheritance and Geometric Golden Mean principles, using artistic and architectural means and processes.

He worked as a designer architect in New York City architectural firms, including Skidmore, Owings and Merrill, Dan Ionescu Architects, Peix and Marchetti Architects, Mihai Radu Architects.

Continuing Education in Fine Arts - Fashion Institute of Technology of NYC 2003

Bachelors in Architecture - University of Bucharest 2001

Bachelors in Math - University of Bucharest 1997
