# Genu

American - born Romanian

In painting, installation, land art and sculpture Genu reflects on concepts like Grand Design Purpose and Fractality, Magnetic Interaction under Field Awareness, Space-Time-Conscience relations, Inception and Transformation of Amorphous into Sign and vice versa, Perishable States of Matter and Mind in the context of Life synergy, Long Life Formulas. His crossdomain educational backgrounds are of strong influence when it comes to designing structures and spaces embedded with Value Inheritance and Geometric Golden Mean principles, using artistic and architectural means and processes.

With Bachelors in Math and Architecture from the University of Bucharest, Genu (Eugen) Berlo continued his education in Fine Arts at Fashion Institute of Technology of NYC. He worked as an architect for some of the New York City's most eminent architectural firms, including world's highly renowned Skidmore, Owings and Merrill (SOM) architectural, planning and engineering company.
