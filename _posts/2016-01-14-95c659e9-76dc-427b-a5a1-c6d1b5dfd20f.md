**Eugen Berlo**

American - born Romanian

In painting, installation, land art and sculpture, using artistic and architectural means and processes, Eugen Berlo reflects on concepts as Bigger Purpose as Value Inheritance, Thought Trajectories and BioMagnetic Interaction under Field Conscience, Inception and Transformation of Nothingness into Sign and vice versa, Perishable States of Mind against Enduring Seeds of Light, Long Life Formulas, The Only 3 Mathematical Rules for Life. 

With Bachelors in Math and Architecture from the University of Bucharest, **E****ugen Berlo** continued his education in Fine Arts at Fashion Institute of Technology of NYC. He worked as an architect for some of the New York City's most eminent architectural firms, including world's highly renowned Skidmore, Owings and Merrill (SOM) architectural, planning and engineering company.
