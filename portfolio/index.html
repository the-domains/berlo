Portfolio

. selected works by Genu Berlo as of June 2017

I see myself as a generalist, an artist embodying a broad range of cultural education and influences from various fields of social and cultural activities, like art, math, architecture, philosophy, music. What most interests me today, is processes of awareness and perception, memory, imagination and other functions related to identity and magic being. I try to be careful about using words, for instance: experiment. I don’t experiment, but rather play with my painting, printing and fabrications, the results being marks of myself, which is in a process of alternative unfolding, peeling, braking old and reassembling new structures, lighting and shading levels of self selves. Almost all human beings have a phony sense of identity. A delusion, a hallucination as to who they are. I am terrible interested in the problem of identity, and I am trying to find out what people mean when they say the word I. It is one of the most fascinating questions. Who do you think you are?. I feel that technological documentation of memory tends to render useless due to oneselfs excessive use and rather off center placement of interests, tools and desires. However, it is part of the big human game of hide and seek. Secondary to printmaking, colours and painting, I play with materials, dear physical objects, and my behavioral habits, as means of exorcising emotion, embed memory, and alter or permit new mindsets to manifest and grow.

_ Genu Berlo
